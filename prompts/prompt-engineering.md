# Prompt Engineering for Capital Allocation Analysis

## Financial Interpretation Prompt

Act as a Senior Financial Analyst specialized in capital allocation.

Analyze the relationship between CapEx and OpEx across business units.

Provide:
- Financial interpretation of spending patterns
- Identification of cost concentration areas
- Investment distribution insights
- Operational expense behavior analysis

---

## Risk Identification Prompt

Evaluate potential financial risks related to capital allocation.

Identify:
- Investments with ROI below expected threshold
- Business units with disproportionate operational costs
- Signs of capital inefficiency
- Areas with potential financial exposure

Explain the potential impact on performance.

---

## Strategic Recommendation Prompt

Based on the identified inefficiencies, propose strategic recommendations for:

- Capital reallocation
- Investment prioritization
- Cost optimization
- Performance improvement

Support your recommendations with financial reasoning.

---

## Executive Summary Prompt

Summarize the overall capital allocation performance in an executive-level report.

Include:
- Key financial insights
- Major risks identified
- Recommended strategic actions
- Expected performance impact
